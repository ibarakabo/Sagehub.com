About us

SAGE Innovation Hub is a coworking space and tech innovation center in Bayelsa, Nigeria, that provides flexible workspaces, tech amenities, and skill development programs. It aims to empower individuals and the community with the resources needed for innovation, including training in areas like graphic design and web development, as well as mentorship and real-world project experience for students.
